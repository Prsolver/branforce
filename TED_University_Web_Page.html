Our Story

TED University embraces an innovative educational approach that combines academic excellence with real-world experience. The new initiative focuses on directly engaging students with the dynamics of the business world, offering them a strong start in their careers.

Our Audience

  • Current and potential students, especially those who value career development.
  • Businesses and corporations interested in offering job opportunities to new graduates.

Our Strategy

Corporate Collaborations:

  • Establishing quota agreements with companies to guarantee jobs for a certain number of graduates.
  • Adapting the curriculum flexibly to meet the needs of companies, ensuring students acquire the skills and knowledge sought by employers.

Educational Programs:

  • Providing flexibility in academic performance criteria, allowing students a broader range of course selections and career exploration.
  • Organizing special internship programs and workshops in collaboration with companies.

Promotion and Communication:

  • Conducting events and social media campaigns that highlight this innovative approach.
  • Sharing success stories of alumni and interviews with business leaders to underscore the importance of real-world connections.

Market Positioning

TED University is positioned as a forward-thinking educational institution, distinctively bridging the gap between academic learning and professional employment. Unlike traditional universities, TED University integrates real-world business engagements into its core educational model, aligning its curriculum with the evolving demands of the global job market.

Differentiation:

The most significant differentiator for TED University lies in its exceptionally effective approach to career development. The university's strategic partnerships with leading corporations and its tailored curriculum uniquely position its graduates for immediate employment opportunities.

Our Culture

TED University's culture is centered on academic excellence, fostering innovation, and encouraging a diverse and inclusive environment. It prioritizes critical thinking and ethical responsibility, preparing students for global challenges. The university culture is collaborative, supportive, and focused on holistic development.
